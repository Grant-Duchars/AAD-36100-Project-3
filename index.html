Video Game Music Recommendations

Vintage Story

Lo-Phi

Vintage Story is one of my favorite games of all time and I think one of a big reasons behind that is the music. It fits the theme of the game so well. From the tense nights in the woods, to the cozy days of building the music always seems to make it better.

Starbound

Curtis Schweitzer, Solatrus

The instrumental score of starbound fits the game perfectly. Whenever I listen to this soundtrack I think I am on an optamisitc space adventure.

Bug Fables

Tristan Alric

Cheery, colorful, happy. Pretty much sums up Bug Fables. The game is a faithful love letter to the original Paper Mario/Mario & Luigi games and the soundtrack is no different.

Crypt of the Necrodancer

Danny Baranowsky

Bitune bumping goodness.

LISA

Widdly 2 Diddly

Just like the game, this one is weird. The soundtrack is full of bizarre, industrial sounds that just create a unique vibe.

Sludge Life

Doseone

This one is definitely closer to the top of my list of favorites. Sludge Life is an upbeat kick heavy soundtrack and every track is a slapper.

Minecraft

C418

Nostalgia filled jams. This album reminds me of childhood, playing Minecraft, exploring, building, having fun. It always cheers me up hearing anything from this soundtrack.

Risk of Rain 2

Chris Christodoulou

Chris Christodoulou is a king. His style is so creative and consistent and I would easily say that Risk of Rain 2 is his best so far.
